Undergraduate Minor

Applied Artificial Intelligence

Learn AI technical skills  ·  For All Majors  ·  Use your creativity and innovate

Whatever your major, AI will be part of your career. Gain a competitive edge with a minor designed to help you integrate AI into your specific field to solve real-world problems. Designed for students from all majors, not just STEM.

The Applied AI minor equips students from all disciplines to build and integrate AI into professional workflows. Through hands-on coursework, you will master technical skills that go far beyond basic prompt engineering. Use your creativity to build a high-impact portfolio.
